FAQs

Answers to frequently asked questions

What is SnapMeasureAI?

SnapMeasureAI is an AI technology that provides 99% accurate body measurements without the need to visit a tailor.

What body measurements does SnapMeasureAI provide?

SnapMeasureAI offers unlimited body measurements using a precise 3D avatar. With seven thousand index points, you can select the exact measurements you need.

Do I need special clothing or equipment?

No special equipment is required. Just take or upload two photos. For the best results, wear tightly fitting clothing and tuck in any loose shirts.

How accurate are the measurements?

Measurements are 99% accurate across the entire body.

How can I test the technology?

You can now try a free demo at demo.snapmeasureai.com. Experience the technology firsthand and see its impressive accuracy!

What is the main goal of SnapMeasureAI?

SnapMeasureAI is tackling the $500 billion problem of annual retail returns, which also has a significant environmental impact. By offering precise measurements, SnapMeasureAI helps users find their perfect fit reliably.
