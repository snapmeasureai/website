How it works

Two photos. One precise 3D model.

No rotating platform. No special lighting. No wearable. Just a smartphone camera and 10 seconds — delivering the kind of body data that was previously locked behind expensive hardware.

Step 01 — Capture

Front photo + side photo

The user takes one photo from the front and one from the side, wearing fitted clothing. No special pose, no controlled environment required. Images are processed and immediately deleted.

Step 02 — Reconstruct

AI builds a 10,000-point 3D model

Our patented neural network — trained on over one million body scans, 400,000 backgrounds, and 90,000 poses — fuses both views into a precise 3D body model within 10 seconds.

Step 03 — Deliver

100+ measurements + 3D export

Results include 100+ body measurements — circumferences, lengths, and ratios across the full body — plus an exportable 3D OBJ file. Any measurement between any points on the model is available on request.

FAQs

Common questions

What body measurements does the scan produce?+

The scan produces a 10,000-point 3D body model, meaning any circumference or length measurement between any body points can be generated. Standard outputs include chest, waist, hips, thighs, biceps, forearms, calves, neck, shoulder width, inseam, and full torso lengths. Custom measurement sets are available for apparel sizing, uniform fitting, fitness tracking, and health monitoring applications.

How does AI body scanning reduce apparel returns?+

Poor fit is the leading cause of apparel returns, accounting for over half of all items sent back. SnapMeasureAI gives shoppers precise body measurements from two smartphone photos in 10 seconds — no tape measure, no guesswork. E-commerce platforms and apparel brands integrate the scan to deliver accurate size recommendations, reducing return rates and increasing purchase confidence at the moment of decision.

How does SnapMeasureAI help GLP-1 weight loss programs?+

GLP-1 medications drive real body shape changes — waist and hip measurements shift, proportions change — but a bathroom scale only shows one number. SnapMeasureAI gives patients and clinicians a precise 3D scan and 100+ measurements that can be compared scan-to-scan over months, making the actual body change visible and measurable. No hardware or clinic visit required.

How can fitness apps and personal training platforms integrate body measurement tracking?+

The scan runs through a web-based interface — users provide two photos and their height, and receive 100+ body measurements plus an optional 3D OBJ model within 10 seconds. Fitness platforms use this to offer clients objective circumference tracking, visual 3D progress comparisons, and muscle gain measurement over time. Enterprise partners can configure custom measurement outputs and white-label experiences.

How accurate is the body scan compared to tape measurements?+

Typical error on major measurements is within half an inch — and often much closer. The technology was built on Cambridge research and trained on over one million body scans, 400,000 backgrounds, and 90,000 body poses. That consistency makes it reliable for apparel sizing, fitness progress tracking, and longitudinal health monitoring alike.

Do users need special equipment or clothing?+

No special equipment. Any smartphone camera works. For best accuracy, fitted clothing is recommended and loose shirts should be tucked in. The technology handles any body type, skin tone, pose variation, and background — no controlled environment is required.

How is user data and privacy handled?+

Photos submitted for scanning are processed immediately and deleted — they are not stored or retained. No personally identifiable information is linked to the scan data. Enterprise customers can discuss custom data handling agreements including on-premise or dedicated cloud deployments.
