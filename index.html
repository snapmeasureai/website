FAQs

Answers to frequently asked questions

What is SnapMeasureAI?

SnapMeasureAI is an AI body scanning technology that generates a high-resolution 3D body model from two photos — front and side — in under 10 seconds. The model produces 100+ precise body measurements for use in apparel sizing, health tracking, fitness apps, and GLP-1 weight loss programs.

What body measurements does SnapMeasureAI provide?

SnapMeasureAI offers unlimited body measurements using a precise 3D body model. With over 10,000 points mapped across the body, custom measurements can be generated between any locations on the model, enabling highly flexible body data and analytics.

Do I need special clothing or equipment?

No special equipment is required and only two photos are needed. For the best results, wear tightly fitting clothing and tuck in any loose shirts.

How accurate are the measurements?

Typically within half an inch on major measurements — and often much closer.

How can I test the technology?

You can now try a free demo at demo.snapmeasureai.com. Experience the technology firsthand and see its impressive accuracy.
